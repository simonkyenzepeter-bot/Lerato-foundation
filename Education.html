Introduction

The Lerato Foundation Education Support Program provides scholarships and guidance to talented, disadvantaged, and community-minded students. We support their academic journey, leadership development, and personal growth, while helping them transition successfully to the next level of education.

1. Program Objectives

  • 🎯Provide financial support to students with limited resources.
  • 🏅Recognize and nurture talents, leadership skills, and commitment to community service.
  • 📈Support smooth academic progression and transition to the next educational level.
  • 💡Mentor students for holistic personal and academic development.

2. Eligibility & Selection

  • 💰Disadvantaged students who demonstrate need and commitment.
  • 🎨Students with exceptional talents in academics, arts, or sports.
  • 🤝Individuals showing leadership skills and active community service.
  • 🎓Students preparing to transition to higher levels of education.

3. Scholarship Support

  • 💵Full or partial tuition coverage depending on need and merit.
  • 📚Provision of learning materials and mentorship guidance.
  • 📝Assistance in academic planning and career development.
  • ➡️Guidance to ensure successful progression to the next educational level.

4. Program Approach

  1. 📝Application & Selection: Transparent selection based on need, talent, leadership, and community service.
  2. 🎯Orientation: Introduction to scholarship program, mentorship, and responsibilities.
  3. 📖Ongoing Support: Academic guidance, mentorship, and counseling throughout the study period.
  4. 🎓Transition Assistance: Support for students advancing to higher educational levels.
  5. 📊Monitoring & Evaluation: Regular progress reports and feedback.

5. Expected Outcomes

  • 🎓Students excelling academically and realizing their potential.
  • 🏅Empowered youth demonstrating leadership and community engagement.
  • ➡️Successful transitions to the next educational level.
  • 🌍A community of skilled and motivated learners giving back to society.

6. How to Get Involved

  • 🧑‍🎓As a Student: Apply for scholarship support and mentorship.
  • 👩‍🏫As a Mentor: Provide guidance, coaching, and encouragement to scholarship recipients.
  • 🤝As a Partner: Contribute resources, funding, or collaborate to support students.
