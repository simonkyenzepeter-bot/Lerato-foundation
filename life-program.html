Our Mission

At Lerato Foundation, we ensure children and communities have access to nutritious meals and safe water. Our mission is to:

  • Eliminate hunger and malnutrition in schools and communities
  • Provide safe, sustainable water sources
  • Empower communities to maintain and sustain these programs long-term
