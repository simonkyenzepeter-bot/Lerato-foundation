Bengo Water Project Launched üö∞

Published on August 19, 2025

Lerato Foundation officially launched the Bengo Water Project on 19th August 2025, a milestone aimed at transforming the lives of families in Bengo and surrounding communities.

This project seeks to drill a borehole, install 4 water tanks, and extend water access points closer to households. Beyond infrastructure, we are committed to training communities on sustainable water harvesting, management, and encouraging agriculture to promote food security.

The Bengo community has long faced water scarcity, forcing children and women to walk long distances in search of clean water. By addressing this need, the Bengo Water Project will not only provide safe water but also restore dignity, promote health, and empower families to thrive.
