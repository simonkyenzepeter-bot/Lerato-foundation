Bengo Water Project

Providing safe water and transforming lives in Bengo community

Problem: Unsafe Water in Bengo

Published on August 19, 2025

The Bengo community has been facing severe water scarcity. Families, especially children and women, rely on unsafe water sources for drinking and daily use, which poses serious health risks and impacts their quality of life.

Solution: Bengo Water Project Launched

On 19th August 2025, Lerato Foundation officially launched the Bengo Water Project. The project aims to drill a borehole, install water tanks, and extend safe water access points closer to households. Community members are also trained on sustainable water management and food security.
