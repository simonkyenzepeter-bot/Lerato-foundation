About Us – Lerato Foundation

Lerato Foundation is a youth-led, student-centered, and community-driven organization founded in November 2022. The foundation began its journey as Unawiri Children Foundation before rebranding to Lerato Foundation to expand its vision and deepen its reach in transforming lives, uplifting communities, and shaping future leaders.

We are guided by the belief that meaningful change starts with students, youth, and communities working together. Students are the seed of tomorrow’s leaders, youth bring innovation and energy, and communities provide the foundation for growth. By bringing these three pillars together, Lerato Foundation drives change that is holistic, sustainable, and impactful.

Our Mission

To nurture leaders, support students, youth, and communities, and empower the next generation through mentorship, education, and sustainable opportunities for growth.

Our Vision

A transformed society where every student, youth, and community has access to resources, dignity, and opportunities to thrive and positively impact the world.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment: Equipping students, youth, and communities with knowledge, skills, and opportunities to succeed.
  • Integrity: Upholding transparency, accountability, and honesty in all our actions.
  • Collaboration: Partnering with schools, youth groups, organizations, and communities to maximize impact.
  • Excellence: Delivering quality in mentorship, programs, and leadership development.
  • Innovation: Continuously adapting initiatives to meet evolving needs.
  • Youth & Student Leadership: Placing students and youth at the heart of solutions that benefit their communities.

Our Key Programs & Initiatives

  • Mentorship Program: Guiding students and youth to discover their talents, make informed decisions, and take leadership roles.
  • Life Program (Nutrition & Clean Water): Providing dignity through food and safe water for healthier living.
  • Education Support Program: Scholarships and opportunities for bright but financially challenged students.
  • Sports Outreach Program: Using sports to engage youth, reduce harmful behaviors, and strengthen resilience.
  • Community Development & Empowerment: Vocational training, health awareness, leadership workshops, and sustainable partnerships.

Why Lerato Foundation?

• We are youth-led, meaning solutions come from those who understand today’s challenges best.
• We are student-centered, recognizing education as the greatest equalizer.
• We are community-driven, ensuring that growth benefits not just individuals but entire families and societies.

At Lerato Foundation, we believe that when students, youth, and communities rise together, society is transformed.
