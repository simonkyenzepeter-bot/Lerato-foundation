About Us

Lerato Foundation, originally founded as Unawiri Children Foundation, is dedicated to transforming lives across Kenya. We focus on mentorship, education, nutrition, and sustainable water solutions to empower children and communities in all 47 counties.

Our Programs

Scholarships & Mentorship

Supporting bright students from all 47 counties through education and mentorship opportunities.

A Meal A Day

Providing daily nutritious meals to learners, ensuring no child studies on an empty stomach.

Life Program – Borehole Drilling

Delivering clean and sustainable water to communities through borehole drilling projects.
