About Us

Lerato Foundation (formerly Unawiri Children Foundation) is a youth-led, community-driven organization transforming lives across Kenya through education, mentorship, nutrition, sports, and community empowerment initiatives. Our focus is on building confident, resilient, and empowered children and youth, while uplifting entire communities to create sustainable change and opportunities.

Our Programs

1. Mentorship Program

We nurture the next generation of leaders by walking with students and young people through structured mentorship. Our mentors provide guidance, role modeling, and capacity building to inspire confidence, resilience, and purpose.

  • Career talks in schools and institutions
  • One-on-one and group mentorship
  • Continuous support in both school and community life
  • Helping youth discover talents and make informed life choices

2. Life Program (Nutrition & Clean Water)

We believe every child deserves the dignity of food and water. The Life Program addresses two vital needs:

  • A Meal A Day (Uji Program): Every Tuesday, Thursday, and Sunday, we provide nutritious porridge to children in schools and communities
  • Clean Water Access: Supporting boreholes and safe water systems for families and communities

This program reduces hunger, promotes health, and restores dignity among vulnerable communities.

3. Education Support Program

Education is key to breaking the cycle of poverty. Lerato Foundation supports deserving students through:

  • Scholarships for bright but financially challenged students
  • Recognizing academic excellence, leadership, talents, and community service
  • Equipping students to succeed in their studies and give back to society

4. Sports Outreach Program

Sports is a powerful tool to empower and protect youth. Through this program, we:

  • Organize sports events that bring youth together
  • Integrate mentorship and life-skills training within sports activities
  • Use sports to reduce drug abuse and harmful behaviors
  • Promote discipline, teamwork, and resilience

5. Community Development & Empowerment Program

Beyond individual support, we strengthen communities through:

  • Vocational and entrepreneurial skills training
  • Community awareness on health, mental well-being, and environmental care
  • Leadership and capacity-building workshops
  • Partnerships with organizations to create sustainable opportunities
