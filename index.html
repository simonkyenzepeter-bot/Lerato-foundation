About Us

Lerato Foundation (formerly Unawiri Children Foundation) is a youth-focused non-profit transforming lives across Africa. We believe every young person deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Through mentorship, education, nutrition, sports, and community programs, we nurture empowered youth and resilient communities, providing scholarships, life skills, clean water, and sustainable development support.
