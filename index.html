About Us

Lerato Foundation is a youth-led, student-centered, and community-driven organization founded in 2022 to empower students, youth, and communities through mentorship, education, sports, and sustainable development programs. We believe in nurturing leaders, creating opportunities, and building resilient societies.

Read More

A Message from Our Founder – Simon Kyenze

"Welcome to Lerato Foundation – formerly known as Unawiri Children Foundation. ‘Lerato’ means love, and love is at the heart of everything we do. Founded in November 2022, our rebranding to Lerato Foundation has expanded our reach, allowing us to transform lives across communities more effectively than ever before."

"Through our mentorship programs, educational support, nutritional initiatives, sports outreach, and community development projects, we create opportunities that uplift individuals, strengthen communities, and build resilient, confident, and capable future leaders."

"We warmly invite you to join us on this journey – whether as a donor, volunteer, or partner. Together, we can make a lasting difference, empower communities, and inspire the next generation to reach heights they never imagined."

"At Lerato Foundation, every contribution, every act of support, and every moment spent with youth matters. Together, let’s ignite hope, unlock potential, and build a brighter future for Africa’s young leaders."

– Simon Kyenze, Founder & Head of Operations, Lerato Foundation
