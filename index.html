About Us – Lerato Foundation

Lerato Foundation (formerly Unawiri Children Foundation) is a youth-focused, community-driven non-profit dedicated to transforming lives across Africa. We believe every child and young person deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of circumstances.

Through mentorship, education, nutrition, sports, and community programs, we nurture empowered youth and resilient communities. Our initiatives provide scholarships, life skills training, clean water access, and sustainable development support, creating lasting, positive impact.

Partner with us to help unlock potential, uplift communities, and build a brighter future for Africa’s next generation.

Our Programs

Mentorship Program

We walk with students and youth through structured mentorship, guiding them to discover talents, make informed choices, and maximize their potential.

  • Career talks in schools
  • One-on-one and group mentorship
  • Continuous support in school and community life

Life Program (Nutrition & Clean Water)

Providing dignity through food and safe water.

  • A Meal A Day (Uji Program) every Tuesday, Thursday, Sunday
  • Clean water access: boreholes & safe water systems

Education Support Program

Scholarships for bright but financially challenged students who excel academically and in leadership and community service.

Sports Outreach Program

Engaging youth through sports integrated with mentorship and life skills to reduce harmful behaviors and build resilience.

Community Development & Empowerment

Strengthening communities through vocational training, health awareness, leadership workshops, and sustainable partnerships.
