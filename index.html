About Us

Lerato Foundation is a youth-led, student-centered, and community-driven organization founded in 2022 to empower students, youth, and communities through mentorship, education, sports, and sustainable development programs.

Read More

A Message from Our Founder – Simon Kyenze

"Welcome to Lerato Foundation – formerly known as Unawiri Children Foundation. ‘Lerato’ means love, and love is at the heart of everything we do..."

"Through our mentorship programs, educational support, nutritional initiatives, sports outreach, and community development projects, we create opportunities that uplift individuals, strengthen communities, and build resilient, confident, and capable future leaders."

"We warmly invite you to join us on this journey – whether as a donor, volunteer, or partner. Together, we can make a lasting difference..."

– Simon Kyenze, Founder & Head of Operations
