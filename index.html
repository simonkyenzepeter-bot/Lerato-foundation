About Us

Who We Are

Lerato Foundation is a youth-led, student-centered, and community-driven organization founded in 2022. We empower students, youth, and communities through mentorship, education, sports, and sustainable development programs.

Our mission is to nurture leaders, support communities, and empower the next generation for lasting change.

Since 2022, Lerato Foundation has been bridging gaps in education, mentorship, and community empowerment — transforming challenges into opportunities for a brighter future.

A Message from Our Founder – Simon Kyenze

"Welcome to Lerato Foundation – formerly Unawiri Children Foundation. ‘Lerato’ means love, and love is at the heart of everything we do. Founded in November 2022, our rebranding expanded our reach, allowing us to transform lives across communities more effectively."

"Through mentorship, education support, nutrition, sports outreach, and community development, we create opportunities that uplift individuals, strengthen communities, and build resilient, confident, and capable future leaders."

"We warmly invite you to join us – as a donor, volunteer, or partner. Together, we can make a lasting difference, empower communities, and inspire the next generation."

– Simon Kyenze, Founder & Head of Operations
